About the artist

I am badclown, a professional software developer, hobby artist and more recently degen NFT trader. I started working as a software dev in 2003, I now run my own dev shop and service some big brands in online learning, ecommerce and medical industries. I am using the NFT space as a way to grow my knowledge of blockchain and web3 early as I have no doubt these technologies will become part of day to day life very soon and it's good to stay ahead of the game. Every feature you see on this page was developed by me as a small taste of what's to come.
